FAQS

Q: How do I change the message that plays when I answer the phone?

A: Click on 'account' and navigate to 'custom message.' Type your custom message in the box and scroll down to select the 'save changes' button.

Q: How do I make more than one phone call a month?

A: You must purchase a pro account to make more than one phone call a month. You can do this under 'accounts'. Select 'upgrade to pro' and enter your credit card information to upgrade to a pro account.

Q: How do I add more than one phone number to my account?

A: If you have a pro account, you can add 2 phone numbers to your account. This is designed so that you can call either your mobile phone or you work phone, to bring you out of a boring conversation wherever you are. You can add a second phone number under 'accounts'. Click on 'add a second number,' add your phone number, and scroll down and press the 'save changes' button. If you do not have a pro account you can also get one under 'accounts'. Select 'upgrade to pro' and enter your credit card information to upgrade to a pro account.
